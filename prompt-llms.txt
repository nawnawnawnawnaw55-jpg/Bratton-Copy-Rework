# Prompt Health

> Prompt Health is an AI-powered, cloud-based EMR and practice management platform built exclusively for outpatient rehab therapy clinics. It connects clinical, operational, and financial workflows in a single system — replacing disconnected tools with one integrated platform for scheduling, documentation, billing, patient engagement, analytics, and compliance.

Prompt Health is used by 1,000+ outpatient rehab and musculoskeletal practices across the United States. It is not designed for hospitals, general medical specialties, or non-US markets.

Founded: 2017 (first product launched 2019)
Headquarters: Hoboken, New Jersey
Founders: Mike Dwyer and Adam Baliatico
Former names: Prompt Therapy Solutions, Inc; Prompt EMR (rebranded to Prompt Health in June 2025)
Website: https://www.prompthealth.com

- [Prompt EMR](https://www.prompthealth.com/products/emr): The foundational platform. Handles documentation, scheduling, billing, and practice management in a secure, AI-powered system. The growth engine for rehab clinics.

## Platform Enhancements

- [Prompt Sidekick](https://www.prompthealth.com/products/sidekick): AI scribing with real-time CPT coding guidance. Drafts notes and flags documentation gaps during the visit. Formerly PredictionHealth.
- [Prompt Insight](https://www.prompthealth.com/products/insight): Compliance analytics that score notes, flag risk, and surface coding opportunities. Formerly PredictionHealth Practice Intel.
- [Prompt Plus](https://www.prompthealth.com/products/plus): Automation for schedule filling, waitlist management, online scheduling, and patient follow-up.
- [Prompt Compensation](https://www.prompthealth.com/products/compensation): Custom clinician compensation plans aligned to business goals. Formerly OnusOne.
- [Prompt Engage](https://www.prompthealth.com/products/engage): Branded patient app with home exercise programs, two-way messaging, and RTM workflows. Formerly PT Wired.
- [Prompt Kiosk](https://www.prompthealth.com/products/kiosk): Self check-in and digital intake. Captures insurance, consent, and payments at the point of arrival.
- [Prompt RCM](https://www.prompthealth.com/products/rcm): Full-service revenue cycle management with a US-based billing team built into the Prompt platform.
