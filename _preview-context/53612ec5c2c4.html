How We Treat Neck Pain

Manual Therapy

Gentle hands-on mobilization of the cervical spine and surrounding muscles to restore movement

Posture Correction

Education on proper positioning at work, home, and during sleep

Therapeutic Exercise

Strengthening deep neck flexors, shoulder stabilizers, and upper back muscles
