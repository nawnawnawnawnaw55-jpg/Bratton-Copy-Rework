Understanding Meniscus Tears

The meniscus is a C-shaped piece of cartilage in your knee that acts as a shock absorber. Tears can occur from:

Acute Injury

Twisting or pivoting with the foot planted — common in sports like football, basketball, and soccer

Degenerative Changes

Gradual wear and tear over time, especially in individuals over 40
