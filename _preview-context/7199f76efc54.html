Labral Tear Rehabilitation

Labral tears — whether in the shoulder or hip — can cause pain, clicking, and instability. Our one-on-one rehabilitation in Slidell helps you recover strength and function, whether you've had surgery or are pursuing conservative management.

Types of Labral Tears We Treat

Shoulder Labral Tears

SLAP tears, Bankart lesions, and posterior labral injuries — common in throwers, swimmers, and after shoulder dislocations

Hip Labral Tears

Anterior, posterior, and superior labral tears — often associated with FAI (femoroacetabular impingement) and sports

Our Approach to Labral Tear Rehab

  • Thorough evaluation of joint stability, range of motion, and mechanics
  • Pain management using manual therapy and modalities
  • Progressive strengthening of rotator cuff or hip stabilizers
  • Post-surgical protocols protecting the repair while restoring function
  • Return-to-sport testing for athletes
  • One-on-one sessions with 45–60 minute appointments
  • No referral needed in Louisiana — Direct Access
