About Bratton Physical Therapy

At Bratton Physical Therapy, we provide one-on-one, hands-on care focused on getting you back to the activities you love — faster and safer. Located in Slidell, Louisiana, our clinic is built on the belief that recovery should never feel rushed or impersonal.

Our Mission

To deliver individualized, evidence-based physical therapy that addresses the root cause of your pain — not just the symptoms. Every patient receives a customized treatment plan designed around their specific goals, lifestyle, and recovery timeline.

What Makes Us Different

  • One-on-One Sessions: You work directly with a licensed physical therapist — never passed off to an aide or tech.
  • Longer Appointments: We schedule 45–60 minute sessions so there's time to listen, assess, and treat thoroughly.
  • No Referral Needed: Louisiana allows Direct Access — you can start treatment without a physician's prescription.
  • Convenient Location: 1346 Lindberg Drive Suite 3, Slidell, LA 70458 — easy access from I-10 and I-12.

Our Approach

We combine manual therapy, therapeutic exercise, and patient education to create lasting results. Whether you're recovering from surgery, managing a chronic condition, or looking to prevent future injuries, we meet you where you are and build a plan that fits.
