Insurance & Billing

We believe cost shouldn't stand between you and recovery. We accept most major insurance plans and offer self-pay options.

Self-pay

No Referral, No Problem

Louisiana allows Direct Access to physical therapy, meaning you can be evaluated and treated without a physician's prescription. Some insurance plans may still require a referral — call us and we'll verify your benefits before your first visit.

Don't See Your Plan?

Insurance networks change frequently. Call us at (985) 641-5825 and our team will verify your coverage and explain your benefits — including copays, deductibles, and visit limits.
