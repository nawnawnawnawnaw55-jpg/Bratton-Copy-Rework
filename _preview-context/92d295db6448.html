Back Conditions We Treat

We help patients recover and rehabilitate from a variety of spine-related conditions:

Herniated & Bulging Discs

Cervical, thoracic, and lumbar disc injuries causing pain, numbness, or weakness

Sciatica

Radiating leg pain from nerve compression in the lower back

Spinal Stenosis

Narrowing of the spinal canal causing pain, cramping, or numbness

Muscle Strains & Sprains

Acute injuries from lifting, twisting, or overexertion

Our Approach to Back Pain

Comprehensive Exam

Detailed assessment of posture, movement patterns, strength, and flexibility

Pain Management

Manual therapy, dry needling, and modalities to reduce pain and muscle tension

Long-Term Prevention

Core strengthening, posture training, and ergonomic education to prevent recurrence
