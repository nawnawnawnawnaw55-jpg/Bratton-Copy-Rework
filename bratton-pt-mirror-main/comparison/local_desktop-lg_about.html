About Us

Bratton Physical Therapy is a physical therapist owned outpatient clinic located in Slidell, Louisiana. We have been in business since 2005.

We offer the most up to date treatment available specializing in all orthopedic injuries from the spine to the extremities.

Our reputation for excellence stands from year to year as satisfied patients refer their friends and family to Bratton Physical Therapy. We provide physical therapy treatment for adolescents and adults, women and men; whether you are an amateur or professional athlete. Our quality of care and expertise will help you lead a more fulfilling and independent life.
