Join Our Team

Bratton Physical Therapy is always looking for talented, passionate professionals to join our team in Slidell, Louisiana. If you're committed to providing exceptional one-on-one patient care, we'd love to hear from you.

Why Work at Bratton PT?

  • One-on-One Care Model: Treat one patient at a time — no double-booking, no aides running your patients.
  • Clinical Autonomy: Make clinical decisions based on your expertise, not productivity quotas.
  • Collaborative Team: Work alongside experienced therapists who share knowledge and support each other.
  • Great Location: Slidell, LA — a growing community on the Northshore with easy access to New Orleans.

Open Positions

We are currently accepting applications for:

  • Licensed Physical Therapists (full-time and part-time)
  • Physical Therapist Assistants
  • Front Office / Patient Coordinator

How to Apply

Send your resume and a brief cover letter to our office. We'll reach out to qualified candidates to schedule an interview.
