Shoulder Pain Treatment

Shoulder pain can make simple tasks — reaching for a cabinet, putting on a shirt, sleeping through the night — feel impossible. At Bratton Physical Therapy, we treat shoulder conditions with one-on-one, hands-on care that targets the root cause, not just the symptoms.

Common Shoulder Conditions We Treat

Rotator Cuff Tears Shoulder Impingement Labral Tears Frozen Shoulder Post-Surgical Rehab

Our Approach

Every shoulder is different. Your treatment plan may include manual therapy to restore joint mobility, targeted strengthening for the rotator cuff and scapular stabilizers, dry needling for muscle tension, and guided exercise progression to return to your activities safely. All delivered one-on-one by a licensed physical therapist.

Why Patients Choose Us for Shoulder Pain

  • One-on-one sessions — your therapist is with you the entire time
  • 45–60 minute appointments for thorough care
  • Advanced techniques including dry needling and cupping
  • No referral needed in Louisiana
